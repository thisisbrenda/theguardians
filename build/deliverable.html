The Deliverable

There are two aspects of The Guardians that I created: the webcomic and the accompanying interface.

The Interactive Webcomic


The Guardians is an interactive web comic that teaches children about their impact on climate change. This is achieved by allowing the user to become the protagonist. They can create their own character and develop their look by reading chapters and engaging in mini games.

The Accompanying Interface


The interface is a supplement to the webcomic. Here, the user can play games to improve their environmental impact, customize their avatar, and brush up on their knowledge about ways in which they can help fight climate change.
